Welcome

Did you ever needed someone to help you to move a couch, explain Fourier transformation, or just someone to drink a beer with? Well you find the perfect place to find these people! Welcome to SmartCampus, an ideal place to solve your problems and help others :). In this article I will guide you through the main functions of the application. These are the following:

  • Creating problems
  • Adding solutions
  • Accepting solutions
  • Ratings
  • Notifications
  • Ignore users
  • Reputation system
Creating problems

Creating problems are the main feature of the web site. To do this you have to click on the plus sign on the navigation bar. After that you have to fill the form and click on the button bellow. It's quite simple isn't it? :) A problem has 7 attributes, these are:

  • Title
  • Place
  • Required people
  • Tags
  • Deadline
  • Description
  • Bounty

The first six attributes are mandatory, while the Bounty is optional. Problems with bounty will appear at the top of the problem list, so you might get faster responses. But beware, the amount of bounty reputation will substract from your reputation.
There are two different kind of problems in the system, the classic problems and the confidental problems. Classic problems are the casual everyday problems, while confidental problems are more sensitive, usually involves money, or other values. In confidental problems you can also define a minimum reputation limit, so only users with high reputation point will be able to leave solution suggestions to your problem. When you reach 1500 reputation point you can create your own confidental problems. :)
After creating a problem you will receive 10 reputation point, and you can create 5 problems hourly.

Adding solutions

Ok, we talked about problems, but what about solutions? When you open a problem you can add your solution to it. Adding a solution is very simple, just set the amount of the served people, and some description.(e.g.: describe your suggestion and add some contact information) If the problems requires more than one man, you can provide that many people in a solution. It is recommended, because you gain more reputation, so bring some friends along! :)

Accepting solutions

If someone offered you a solution you can accept it by by hitting the clicking the button under the suggestion. By accepting a solutions the amount of the required people the solve the problem will decrease by the amount of the people that the solution serve. When the amount of the required people will hit zero, the problem will be closed, and after it's deadline time plus one hour passes ratings will appear on every user's page who contributed to solving the problem.

Ratings

After the meeting, you can rate the other users who helped you, and they will rate you as well. You can open your pending ratings them by clicking the star sign on the navigation bar. Rating is pretty simple you just have answer 4 questions about a meeting, and the helping and you are done! After you submit your rating the system will calculate a reputation point to the other user, who helped you.

Reputation system

In the previous point I wrote a lot about reputation point. Probably you wonder why the hack we need these? To understand it you have to think about it, will you rather accept a solution from a new user, who just registered and you have no guarantee to show up and actually help you (i could be also just a bot...), or will you rather accept help from a trusted veteran who already helped a lot of people?. So the answer is pretty simple: it represent the user's reliability.
